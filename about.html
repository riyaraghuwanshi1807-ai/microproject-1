About LifeDrop

Our mission, vision, and the passionate team behind the cause

Our Mission

LifeDrop was founded with a single goal — to bridge the gap between blood donors and patients in need. Every year, millions of people require blood transfusions due to accidents, surgeries, and medical conditions.

We believe that no one should lose their life due to a shortage of blood. With a network of thousands of registered donors across India, we ensure that help is always just a call away.

Since 2020, we have facilitated over 4.5 million life-saving donations and continue to grow our community of heroes every single day.

Saving Lives Since 2020

Over 4.5 million lives saved through our platform across 48 cities in India.
