About LifeDrop

Our mission, vision, and the team behind the cause

Our Mission

LifeDrop was founded with a single goal — to bridge the gap between blood donors and patients in need. Every year, millions of people require blood transfusions due to accidents, surgeries, and medical conditions. We make it easy to find donors and save lives.

We believe that no one should lose their life due to a shortage of blood. With a network of thousands of registered donors across India, we ensure that help is always just a call away.
