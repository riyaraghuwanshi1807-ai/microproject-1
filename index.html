Eligibility

Can You Donate?

Check if you meet the basic requirements to become a blood donor.

You Can Donate

  • Age between 18–65 years
  • Weight above 50 kg
  • Hemoglobin ≥ 12.5 g/dL
  • No major illness in last 6 months
  • Not donated in last 3 months
  • Good general health

You Cannot Donate

  • Pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Have HIV, Hepatitis B or C
  • Recent surgery or tattoo (6 months)
  • Taking blood thinners
  • Low blood pressure or anemia
  • Recent travel to malaria zones
